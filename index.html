1st Reliability Quantification for LLMs Workshop @AAAI

Quantifying Reliability in Language Models

Date: January, 2026

Location: AAAI 2026, Singapore EXPO

Status: Under Review

Welcome to the 1st Reliability Quantification for LLMs Workshop at AAAI 2026.

This workshop focuses on developing methods and metrics for quantifying reliability in Large Language Models.

Workshop Goals

  • Advance understanding of LLM reliability quantification
  • Develop practical metrics and evaluation frameworks
  • Foster collaboration between researchers and practitioners
  • Address challenges in high-stakes applications

Important Information

We invite submissions on uncertainty quantification, reliability metrics, robustness, and applications of reliable LLMs in critical domains.

Submission details and important dates will be announced soon.
