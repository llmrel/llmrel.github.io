1st Reliability Quantification for LLMs Workshop @AAAI

Quantifying Reliability in Language Models

Date: January, 2026

Location: AAAI 2026, Singapore EXPO

Status: Under Review

Welcome to the 1st Reliability Quantification for LLMs Workshop at AAAI 2026. This workshop focuses on developing methods and metrics for quantifying reliability in Large Language Models.

Our workshop aims to bring together researchers and practitioners to discuss challenges and solutions in making Large Language Models more reliable and trustworthy.

Important Dates

Submission deadline and other important dates will be announced soon.

Topics of Interest

We welcome submissions on topics including but not limited to:

  • Uncertainty quantification in LLMs
  • Reliability metrics and evaluation methods
  • Robustness testing and validation
  • Trustworthy AI and model interpretability
  • Error detection and correction mechanisms
